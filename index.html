Purpose of the Survey

The primary aim of the customer satisfaction survey is to assess how well our products and services meet the needs and expectations of our customers. By collecting and analyzing feedback, we can:

  • Understand customer perceptions and satisfaction levels.
  • Identify strengths and weaknesses in our offerings.
  • Gather suggestions for improvements and new features.
  • Monitor changes in customer satisfaction over time.
